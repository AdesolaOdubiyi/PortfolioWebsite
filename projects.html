Aspire Active

A gym website created by my group in our final week to showcase what we learned during our 6 weeks at All Star Code. This website is a cumulation of both our coding knowledge and personal interests. This website utilizes an API in order to give users information about what workouts they can do to achieve their goals based on muscle group. I plan to transform this website by using Artificial Intelligence in order to automatically generate a workout routine for users after they complete a survey that asks about their personal fitness goals.



Ablehealthcare

Ablehealthcare is a private psychiatric practice specializing in psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and personalized treatment plans for individuals with behavioral and mental health disorders. I played a key role in building the practice from the ground up, managing tasks ranging from designing logos and creating business cards to streamlining patient interactions through software like IntakeQ. I also handled web development, using WordPress and navigating web hostings. Together with the doctor, we successfully booked over 50 patients, with 25+ recurring clients, and garnered 15 five-star reviews, generating $13,000+ in revenue within 10 months.
